Pasta Gnocchi

About this recipe

Pasta Gnocchi is a great weeknight meal featuring a simple red sauce.

Ingredients
  • Gnocchi
  • Canned Tomatos
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmasean Cheese
Steps
  1. Cut onion in half and add to pot
  2. Add canned salt, tomatos and butter
  3. Bring to simmer stirring occasionally
  4. Once reduced to desired consistency, add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Bring salted water to a boil and cook Gnocchi until they float to the top
  6. Add cooked Gnocchi to sauce and fold with parmasean cheese
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve warm
