Parmasean Risotto

About this recipe

This Risotto recipie requires patience but this creamy pasta dish is worth it in the end.

Ingredients
  • Arborio Rice
  • Chicekn Stock
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parmasean Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
Steps
  1. Bring chicken stock to a slow simmer
  2. In another pot, melt butter and sautee garlic untill fragrant
  3. Add Arborio Rice and mix ingredients well
  4. Slowly laddle stock into pot with ingrediants mixing until the rice abosrbs all the liquid
  5. Repeat the previous step until all the stock is gone
  6. Fold in parmasean cheese and butter
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve warm
